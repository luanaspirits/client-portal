How To Use This

A simple daily rhythm

  1. Pull a card each morning, or any time you'd like guidance. You can pull again as many times as you like, use this as your daily card pull, or as a mini reading for yourself whenever you need one.
  2. Check off your daily practice as you move through the day. It's less about doing all five, and more about noticing where you already are.
  3. Answer the weekly reflection questions on paper in a journal, or right here in the boxes below.
  4. Before you close this page, save your entry. Tap the gold button at the bottom to download today's card, checklist, and reflection as a PDF, and keep it somewhere safe as your own healing journal.

Nothing on this page saves automatically. Once you leave or refresh, it resets, so download your entry first if you want to keep it.
