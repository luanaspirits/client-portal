"Not Chosen" Interrupt Tool
For the moment the despair or comparison starts
0/5 ▼

Use this the moment you notice it starting, a friend feels off, someone else's timeline looks faster than yours, a role goes to someone else. Work through these one at a time.

1. Notice: "Where do I feel this in my body right now?"
For you, this tends to land in your chest and throat. Just locate it, no need to change it yet.
2. Validate: "This feeling is valid, even if I can't fully explain it in words"
3. Name it: "This is the 'not chosen' wound talking, not necessarily today's truth"
4. Permission: "I give myself full permission to fail and pivot, as many times as I need to"
Straight from your own words on the call, failure is just another data point, not a verdict on your worth.
5. Choose: "What would the version of me who already trusts her worth do right now?"
Not the version scanning for threat. The version already standing in her North Star.
